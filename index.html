Alberto Santos Dumont

An aeronaut and inventor, born in 1873 in Minas Gerais, Brazil. He is mostly recognized for his pioneering in building and piloting aerial vehicles like the 14-bis. His aviation career ended in 1910, after the winning of several competitions, mainly in France. With his health deteriorating, facing accusations of being a german spy and seeing aerial vehicles used at wars, he got seriously depressed. He died in 1932.
